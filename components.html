Usage Guidelines

‚úì Do

  • Use consistent image aspect ratios (4:3 recommended)
  • Display relevant certifications prominently
  • Keep supplier names concise and readable
  • Use status badges sparingly (1 per product)
  • Ensure product images are high quality

‚úó Don't

  • Overcrowd cards with too many badges
  • Use low-resolution or stretched images
  • Mix different card sizes in the same grid
  • Hide important certification information
  • Use vague product titles
